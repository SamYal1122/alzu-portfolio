About Me

Who Am I?

I am a very passionate girl with an insatiable curiosity about the world around me. As an educator, I believe in the transformative power of learning and the profound impact a teacher can have on shaping young minds.

My journey has been one of continuous growth, exploration, and self-discovery. I see teaching not just as a profession, but as a calling to inspire and nurture the next generation of thinkers and leaders.

"A teacher not only teaches but builds the future." This philosophy guides everything I do in my professional and personal life.

My Journey

Early Inspiration

My passion for teaching began early, inspired by my own teachers who saw potential in me that I didn't yet see in myself.

"Every child is a seed waiting to blossom. As educators, we provide the sunlight, water, and nourishment for that growth."

Formal Education

Pursued my academic journey with dedication, completing my SEE, +2 Science, and BBS while developing my teaching philosophy.

"Education is not just about acquiring knowledge, but about learning how to think, question, and understand the world."

Montessori Training

Completed comprehensive Montessori training at Excellence Montessori, mastering child-centered educational approaches.

"The Montessori method showed me that education should follow the child's natural development, not force the child to follow rigid systems."

Teaching Experience

Applied my knowledge and training in real classroom settings, discovering the joys and challenges of guiding young minds.

"Every day in the classroom is an opportunity to make a difference. The light in a child's eyes when they understand something new is my greatest reward."

Future Vision

Continuing to grow as an educator while exploring innovative teaching methods and expanding my impact.

"I believe learning never ends. My journey continues as I seek new ways to inspire and be inspired, to teach and to learn."
